Samply is your professional home for audio. Upload tracks in any format, organize them into projects, and share them with exactly who you want — whether that's a mix client, a collaborator across the world, or fans ready to buy. You stay in full control of who can listen, download, comment, and pay.

## Get started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up at [samply.app](https://samply.app). No credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a project and add tracks">
    Click **New project** in the sidebar, then drag and drop your audio files onto it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share with listeners">
    Click **Share** to invite collaborators by email or generate a public link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect feedback">
    Listeners can leave time-coded comments directly on your tracks.
  </Step>
</Steps>
