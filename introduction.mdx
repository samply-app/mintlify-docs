## Who Samply is for

Samply is designed for anyone who works professionally with audio — producers, mixing and mastering engineers, composers, sound designers, and musicians. If you send audio files to other people as part of your work, Samply replaces scattered emails, generic cloud storage links, and consumer streaming tools with a purpose-built experience that reflects the quality of your craft.

## Core value propositions

**Connect with your listeners.** Invite clients, collaborators, or fans to a project and give each person exactly the level of access they need — listener, editor, or admin.

**Accept payments.** Charge clients for deliverables without leaving the platform. Samply integrates with Stripe so you can get paid as part of the sharing workflow.

**Save time sharing tracks.** Stop re-uploading files and re-sending links every revision. Samply keeps everything in one place and lets you update a project without disrupting your listeners' access.

**Integrate with your tools.** Use the Samply API to automate uploads, trigger notifications, or embed the player in your own site or app.
