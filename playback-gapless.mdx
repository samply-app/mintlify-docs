Samply plays back your tracks gaplessly, stitching adjacent tracks in the sequence into one contiguous audio stream. This is essential when you're previewing projects that contain crossfades, segues, or any other seamless transitions — every edit is presented exactly as intended, with no interruptions between tracks.

## How Gapless Playback Works

Because Samply is a collaborative, real-time platform, the app responds immediately to any changes made to a project's track list while it is being played. This live reactivity introduces one narrow exception: gapless transitions are **temporarily disabled** while the track list is actively changing.

This means that if a collaborator adds or reorders a track during playback, the seamless stitching pauses briefly until the track list stabilizes. Once the list stops changing, gapless playback resumes normally.

## iOS App

<Note>
  The native Samply iOS app has virtually no exceptions to gapless playback. It is always gapless and is generally more robust than browser-based playback environments. If uninterrupted, seamless playback is critical to your review process, use the iOS app for the most reliable experience.
</Note>
