Samply gives you full control over who can hear your work. Everything is private by default, so your tracks stay protected until you decide otherwise — you choose exactly how much access each person gets and can adjust it at any time.

## Private Projects

Private projects are the most secure sharing option. Before anyone can access your files, they must create a free Samply account and verify their email address, ensuring you always know who is listening.

### Invite via Email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Share Dialog">
    Tap the **Share** button in your project, or press `.` on your keyboard to open the Share Dialog instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter an email address">
    Type your invitee's email address into the input field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a role">
    Choose the appropriate access level from the role dropdown (see the table below).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invite">
    Press **Enter** to send the invitation. Once accepted, the contact is saved to your address book for faster access in the future.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Role Permissions

Each invited collaborator is assigned one of three roles. Use the table below to choose the right level of access for each person.

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Admin** | Delete projects, edit artwork, edit project name, invite others |
| **Editor** | Upload files, delete files, rename files, edit link options |
| **Listener** | Listen to all files in the project; additional access (comments, downloads, versions) is subject to Access Options |

### Invite Links

If you don't have a collaborator's email address, generate an **Invite Link** instead. This link is single-use — it expires as soon as someone uses it, keeping your project secure.

### Access Requests

If someone receives your private project link but hasn't been granted access, they see an **Access Denied** screen with an option to request access. You receive a notification and can choose to approve or deny the request.

## Public Projects

Setting a project to **Public** lets listeners start playing immediately — no account required. Use this when convenience matters more than restricting who can listen.

### Access Options

Fine-tune your listeners' experience through the **Options Panel**. The following access options are available for public projects:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Comments" icon="message" href="/comments">
    Allow or disable commenting on shared links.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Downloads" icon="arrow-down-to-line" href="/sharing">
    Prevent listeners from downloading your tracks while still allowing playback.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Versions" icon="clock-rotate-left" href="/sharing">
    Restrict version switching so listeners only hear the latest version.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Passwords" icon="lock" href="/sharing">
    Add a password for an extra layer of security on public links.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payments" icon="credit-card" href="/payments">
    Set up a paywall to gate access or downloads behind a payment.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  When using a password on a public link, always share the password through a separate channel — avoid sending both the link and password in the same message.
</Note>

## Additional Links

Beyond the primary project link, you can generate dedicated links for specific files, folders, or collections of items. Each additional link is independent and carries its own access options, so you can give different people different levels of access to the same project.

Manage all your links in the **Options Panel** under the **Links** section. From there you can deactivate or delete any link at any time.
