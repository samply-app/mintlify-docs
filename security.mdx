Samply is built to be a secure, professional environment for sharing work-in-progress audio. Grammy-winning engineers, producers, and artists trust Samply to manage their most sensitive sessions. Every feature is designed with privacy as a baseline — your files are private by default, and you decide exactly who can access them.

## Access Control

Every file you upload to Samply is **private by default**. Nothing is accessible to anyone until you explicitly share it.

## Data Encryption

Samply uses enterprise-grade encryption at every layer of data storage and transfer:

- **At rest** — All stored data is protected with **AES-256** encryption at the storage layer.
- **In transit** — Data is encrypted before transmission, authenticated at both endpoints, and decrypted only on verified arrival.
- **Passwords** — All passwords are hashed using modern cryptographic algorithms. Your password is never stored or transmitted in plaintext.

## Secure Sign-In

You can sign in with **Apple** or **Google** for a faster, more secure login experience. Both providers support their own forms of two-factor authentication and biometric verification — including Apple's **Face ID** — so you benefit from those protections automatically.

## Two-Factor Authentication

Enroll in two-factor authentication from your [account preferences](/profile) for an extra layer of sign-in security. Once enabled, Samply sends a 6-digit SMS code to your device each time you sign in. You'll need to enter that code in addition to your password.

## Right to Be Forgotten

If you choose to leave Samply, you can permanently delete your account and all associated personal data from our servers. This action is irreversible — see the [Profile](/profile) page for instructions.
