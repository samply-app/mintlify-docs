# Source: https://docs.samply.app/profile.html

Your Samply profile is your identity on the platform. Access it via the [Navigation Panel](https://docs.samply.app/./overview.html#navigation-panel) or use the shortcut `G` then `M`.

From your profile, you can update both your [personal information](https://docs.samply.app/#personal-info) and your [brand](https://docs.samply.app/#brand-info).

## Personal Info [​](https://docs.samply.app/#personal-info)

![Profile View](https://docs.samply.app/img/profile/profile.png)

You can edit the following details:

- **Profile photo**
- **Display name**
- **Email**

> _Note:_ Updates may take a few minutes to reflect across the app.

## Custom Branding [​](https://docs.samply.app/#custom-branding)

![Brand View](https://docs.samply.app/img/profile/branding.png)

Configure your brand across the links you share by setting:

- **Brand Image**
- **Brand Name**
- **Optional Description**
 - Supports HTML for linking websites or social accounts

## Account Management [​](https://docs.samply.app/#account-management)

### Reset Password [​](https://docs.samply.app/#reset-password)

You can reset your password by requesting a reset link. This link will be sent to the email associated with your account, allowing you to set a new password securely.

### Two-Factor Authentication [​](https://docs.samply.app/#two-factor-authentication)

Enhance your account security by enabling **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**. Once activated, logging in will require both your password and a secondary verification code sent to your device.

### Delete Account [​](https://docs.samply.app/#delete-account)

Permanently delete your Samply account along with all associated data. This action is irreversible, so be sure to back up any important files before proceeding.
