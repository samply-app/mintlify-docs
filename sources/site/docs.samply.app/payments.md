# Payments [​](https://docs.samply.app/#payments)

You can add paywalls to accept payment from clients, collaborators or fans. If you haven't setup your account to handle payments yet, follow [this guide](https://docs.samply.app/stripe.html).

## Paywall configurations [​](https://docs.samply.app/#paywall-configurations)

Payments can be configured to selectively unlock **downloads** or **access** to your content once a payment for a specific price has been made. The unlock you choose applies either to the individual **purchaser only** or **everyone** with the link.

## Adding a paywall [​](https://docs.samply.app/#adding-a-paywall)

To add a paywall, expand the payments section of the options sidebar and enter the price you wish to use.

INFO

All prices will use the default currency associated with your Stripe account.

Then, use the dropdowns to select if downloads or access should unlock for the purchaser only or for everyone. Once these are selected, click "Set paywall" to add the paywall.

## Editing a paywal [​](https://docs.samply.app/#editing-a-paywal)

To edit a paywall, expand the payments section and click the edit icon. This will allow you to change the price, what the paywall unlocks, and who the paywall unlock applies to.

## Removing a paywal [​](https://docs.samply.app/#removing-a-paywal)

To remove a paywall, expand the payments section and click the minus icon. This will completely remove the paywall.

INFO

Once a paywall is removed, permissions will be determined by the link options (download and access).

## Testing [​](https://docs.samply.app/#testing)

Since the project owner and collaborators automatically bypass paywall restrictions, you can test what your client would see by opening the link in an incognito window (signed out).
