# Frequently Asked Questions [​](https://docs.samply.app/#frequently-asked-questions)

### How do I update project settings? [​](https://docs.samply.app/#how-do-i-update-project-settings)

Open the [project](https://docs.samply.app/./projects.html) you want to edit. From there, options such as **downloads**, **comments**, and **passwords** will be accessible via the **right** **sidebar**.

![](https://docs.samply.app/img/player-options.jpeg)

**Note:** If it's not already open, click the icon at the top right of your screen:

![](https://docs.samply.app/img/player-sidebar.jpeg)

### How do I reorder tracks? [​](https://docs.samply.app/#how-do-i-reorder-tracks)

Reordering tracks is as easy as drag and drop. ![](https://docs.samply.app/img/reorder-tracks.gif)

### How do I download files? [​](https://docs.samply.app/#how-do-i-download-files)

If the project has downloads enabled, a large download button will be visible. If there is no download button, reach out to the project owner. ![](https://docs.samply.app/img/download-files.jpeg)

### Playback is lossy. How can I ensure lossless playback? [​](https://docs.samply.app/#playback-is-lossy-how-can-i-ensure-lossless-playback)

On the project, click the Audio Options button and switch the Lossless toggle to on. ![](https://docs.samply.app/img/enable-lossless.gif)

### How do I delete a project? [​](https://docs.samply.app/#how-do-i-delete-a-project)

Proceed with caution! Deleting a project cannot be undone. You can delete a project from your Library view. ![](https://docs.samply.app/img/delete-project.gif)

### How do I add a collaborator to a project? [​](https://docs.samply.app/#how-do-i-add-a-collaborator-to-a-project)

Collaborators can be added with the share button. ![](https://docs.samply.app/img/add-collaborator.gif)

### How do I see who has viewed my project? [​](https://docs.samply.app/#how-do-i-see-who-has-viewed-my-project)

As the owner of a project, you can view listeners from the right sidebar. ![](https://docs.samply.app/img/view-analytics.jpeg)

### How do I change the color of my project? [​](https://docs.samply.app/#how-do-i-change-the-color-of-my-project)

You can change the color of your project from the right sidebar. ![](https://docs.samply.app/img/change-color.jpeg)
