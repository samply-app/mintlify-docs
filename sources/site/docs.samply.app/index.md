# Samply [​](https://docs.samply.app/#samply)

Our mission is **to enable more people to make a living with audio.** Samply allows you to connect with your listeners, whether they are clients, collaborators, or fans. You have complete control over how you share your audio.

Get the most value out of Samply by [accepting payments](https://docs.samply.app/./payments.html), integrating with our [API](https://docs.samply.app/./api/introduction.html), or simply by saving time when [sharing your tracks](https://docs.samply.app/./sharing.html).

## docs. [​](https://docs.samply.app/#docs)

This site (**docs.samply.app**) is intended to be an exhaustive account of everything that can be done in Samply and how to do it. It's still a **work-in-progress**. If you can't find what you are looking for please don't hesitate to reach out to us directly.

We're a small team of developers from [Wisconsin](https://samply.app/about). You can reach us via the in-app chat Monday through Friday.
