# Comments [​](https://docs.samply.app/#comments)

Comments are a great way to take notes or get feedback on your tracks. In this article, we'll learn how to:

- Open the comment view from your project's files
- From a player
- Leave a general comment
- Leave a time-coded comment
- Format your comment

### Open the comment view [​](https://docs.samply.app/#open-the-comment-view)

To comment, open the comment view (also known as your **File Preview**). If the song you want to comment on is currently playing, just click on it's title in the **Now Playing** bar.

[![](https://storage.crisp.chat/users/helpdesk/website/9ddd1ac4825a5000/d685abfb-12c7-41de-946e-61345c_vxw53o.gif)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/663481555/666bfb1adf9ee9a21069a0ed/CleanShot+2023-01-31+at+19.36.33.gif)

You can also open the **File Preview** for tracks that aren't currently playing. This looks a little different between your **Project** **Files** and from a **Player**.

### From your project files [​](https://docs.samply.app/#from-your-project-files)

To open the **Preview** for a **File** in your project, simply click on the file itself. If there are already comments on the file you can click on that "wafer" as well. [![](https://storage.crisp.chat/users/helpdesk/website/cdce2526ec118000/14339369-40a8-4083-80af-39fe34_hmebm9.gif)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/663484891/0cc70545a4bc1899bc2a8833/CleanShot+2023-01-31+at+19.48.37.gif)

### Format your comment [​](https://docs.samply.app/#format-your-comment)

You can also format your comments by using the **Bold** and _Italic_ modifiers (Command + B, and Command + I).

### Download comments as a file [​](https://docs.samply.app/#download-comments-as-a-file)

You can download all comments as a text file. First, use (Command + K) to open up the command palette. Then select "Download comments". Your download will appear in your downloads folder shortly.
