# Library [​](https://docs.samply.app/#library)

The Library view in Samply provides quick access to your projects and any links shared with you. It allows you to organize and browse your content efficiently. You can filter and sort to find what you need quickly.

![A populated Library](https://docs.samply.app/img/library/library.png)

## Opening the Library View [​](https://docs.samply.app/#opening-the-library-view)

Navigate to the Library by clicking on the link in [Navigation Panel](https://docs.samply.app/./overview.html#navigation-panel). Alternatively you can use the keyboard shortcut `G` then `L` to navigate there quickly.

## Filtering [​](https://docs.samply.app/#filtering)

To filter your library, click on one of the filter options at the top left.

- **Mine**: Projects that you own
- **Shared With Me**: Projects that you are a collaborator and any links shared with you
- **Favorites**: Any item marked as "Favorite"

Additionally, you can filter your library by searching for the item. Click the search icon at the top right of the Library or use the shortcut `⌘` `F` and type in a query.

## Sorting [​](https://docs.samply.app/#sorting)

To sort your Library, choose a sort criteria from the dropdown menu at the top right. For each criteria you can choose to sort in ascending or descending order.

## Favoriting [​](https://docs.samply.app/#favoriting)

You can mark any item in your Library as a favorite to help make it easier to access. Favorite items will be pinned to the [Navigation Panel](https://docs.samply.app/./overview.html#navigation-panel) and can easily be found by [filtering](https://docs.samply.app/#filtering) for them.

To favorite an item in your library, right click and choose "Toggle favorite" from the popout menu. Alternatively, you can select the item or items you want to favorite and hit `F` on your keyboard.
