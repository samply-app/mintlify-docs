Before you can accept payments through Samply, you need to connect a Stripe account. Stripe handles all payment processing, and Samply guides you through the onboarding flow directly from your integrations page. The setup may require multiple visits to the Stripe portal depending on the information Stripe needs to verify your account.

## Setting Up Stripe

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Stripe Payments">
    In the sidebar, go to **Integrations → Stripe Payments** and click **Create account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email in Stripe">
    Stripe will ask for an email address. If the email you enter is already associated with an existing Stripe account, you can choose to connect that account or create a new one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Stripe to process">
    Stripe may need time to process your information before it can finalize your account. If this happens, you'll be returned to Samply and your account will show a **Continue setup** status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete any additional steps">
    If Stripe requires more information, Samply will display a **Continue setup** button. Click it to return to the Stripe portal. You may need to visit the Stripe portal more than once to satisfy all requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm completion">
    Once setup is complete, an account summary replaces the setup prompts. This summary shows your default currency and any applicable transaction fees based on your subscription.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After Setup

Return to **Integrations → Stripe Payments** at any time to open your Stripe dashboard and manage your account, view payouts, or update your payout details.

To learn how to configure payments on individual projects, visit the [Payments](/payments) page.

<Note>
  Even after completing setup, Stripe may occasionally ask for additional information due to changes in their business verification requirements. When this happens, Samply will prompt you to revisit the Stripe portal. If you run into any issues during setup, reach out via the in-app chat and the team will help you resolve them.
</Note>
