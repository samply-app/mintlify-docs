## iOS

The Samply iOS app is free and available to everyone — both creators and listeners. It's built to deliver the best possible playback experience on mobile, with features that go beyond what a browser can offer.

**Key benefits:**

- **Offline listening** — download projects and play them back without a connection
- **AirPlay support** — stream audio to speakers, TVs, and other AirPlay devices
- **CarPlay support** — control playback safely while driving

[Download on the App Store →](https://apple.co/47Raaty)

## macOS

The Samply desktop app for macOS brings a few meaningful improvements over using Samply in a browser tab.

- **Always in your dock** — launch Samply instantly without hunting for a browser tab
- **Fewer keyboard shortcut conflicts** — Samply's shortcuts work reliably without colliding with browser-level defaults
- **Smoother file downloads** — download tracks and projects more consistently than the browser download experience allows

[Download Samply for macOS →](https://samply.app/install)

## Web Application (PWA)

The full Samply application is installable as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on almost any device. A PWA looks and behaves like a native app — it gets its own icon on your home screen or desktop and opens without a browser toolbar.

### Install on Android

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Samply in Chrome">
    Visit [samply.app](https://samply.app) in the Chrome browser on your Android device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the browser menu">
    Tap the **three-dot menu** in the top-right corner of Chrome.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Home screen">
    Tap **Add to Home screen** and confirm when prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Chrome may prompt you automatically to install Samply when you visit the site. If you don't see the prompt, follow the manual steps above.
</Note>
