Your Samply profile controls how you appear on the platform and how your brand looks to people you share audio with. Access your profile at any time via the Navigation Panel, or press `G` then `M` as a keyboard shortcut. From here you can update personal information, set up custom branding for your shared links, and manage your account security.

## Personal Info

Edit the following details from the Personal Info section of your profile:

- **Profile photo** — Upload an image to represent your account.
- **Display name** — Set the name shown to collaborators and recipients.
- **Email** — Update the address associated with your account.

<Note>
  Changes to your personal info may take a few minutes to reflect across all areas of the app.
</Note>

## Custom Branding

Control how your brand appears on links you share with clients and collaborators. Configure:

- **Brand image** — A logo or image displayed on your shared links.
- **Brand name** — The name shown at the top of shared project pages.
- **Description** — An optional field that supports basic HTML, so you can include clickable links to your website or social media profiles.
