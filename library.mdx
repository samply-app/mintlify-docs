The Library is your home base in Samply — it gives you quick access to every project you own and any links others have shared with you. You can filter, search, sort, and favorite items so the content you need is always within reach.

## Opening the Library

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Library in the Navigation Panel">
    Select the **Library** link in the [Navigation Panel](/overview#navigation-panel) to open the view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Or use the keyboard shortcut">
    Press `G` then `L` to jump straight to the Library from anywhere in the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Filtering

Use the filter options at the top-left of the Library to narrow down what's displayed.

| Filter | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Mine** | Projects you own |
| **Shared With Me** | Projects where you're a collaborator, plus any links shared with you |
| **Favorites** | All items you've marked as a favorite |

## Searching

To search within the Library, click the search icon at the top-right or press `⌘ F`, then type your query. Results update as you type, filtering the list to matching projects and files.

## Sorting

Click the sort dropdown at the top-right of the Library to choose a sort criterion. For each criterion, toggle between ascending and descending order to arrange your content exactly how you want it.

## Favoriting

Marking an item as a favorite pins it to the [Navigation Panel](/overview#navigation-panel) and makes it instantly accessible via the **Favorites** filter.

To favorite one or more items, use either of these methods:

- **Right-click** on an item and choose **Toggle favorite** from the context menu.
- **Select** the item or items you want to favorite, then press `F` on your keyboard.

<Tip>
  Select multiple projects at once and press `F` to favorite them all in a single action — useful when you're onboarding a batch of new work.
</Tip>
