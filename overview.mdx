Samply's interface is built to be efficient for power users while staying accessible to newcomers. Three core elements — the Toolbar, Navigation Panel, and Options Panel — appear throughout the app and give you consistent control no matter which view you're working in.

## Toolbar

The Toolbar runs along the top of the application. Its contents adapt to the view you're currently in, surfacing the controls most relevant to your current task. Check the Toolbar first whenever you're looking for view-specific actions like switching modes or triggering uploads.

## Navigation Panel

The Navigation Panel sits on the left side of the application. Use it to move between views, jump to favorite projects, and access your Library.

<Note>
  If the Navigation Panel is hidden, click the icon at the top-left of the screen or press `[` on your keyboard to open it.
</Note>

## Options Panel

The Options Panel appears on the right side of any project you have edit access to. From here you can configure all project-level settings, including artwork, accent color, and access permissions.

<Note>
  If the Options Panel is hidden, click the icon at the top-right of the screen or press `]` on your keyboard to open it.
</Note>
