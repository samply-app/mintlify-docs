Paywalls let you accept payment from clients, collaborators, or fans before they can access your files or download your tracks. You can configure exactly what a payment unlocks and who it unlocks it for, giving you fine-grained control over how your audio is monetized.

<Note>
  Before you can add a paywall, you must connect your Stripe account. Follow the [Stripe integration guide](/stripe) to get set up first.
</Note>

## Paywall Configurations

When you create a paywall, you choose two things:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="What it unlocks" icon="lock-open" href="/payments">
    **Downloads** — payment unlocks the ability to download files.

    **Access** — payment unlocks the ability to listen to the project at all.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Who it unlocks for" icon="users" href="/payments">
    **Purchaser only** — the unlock applies exclusively to the person who paid.

    **Everyone** — once anyone pays, the link unlocks for all visitors.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Add a Paywall

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the payments section">
    Open the **Options Panel** for your project or link, then expand the **Payments** section in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your price">
    Enter the price you want to charge in the price field.

    <Note>
      All prices use the default currency associated with your Stripe account. To change your default currency, update it directly in your Stripe dashboard.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose what and who to unlock">
    Use the dropdowns to select whether the payment unlocks **Downloads** or **Access**, and whether that unlock applies to the **Purchaser only** or to **Everyone** with the link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate the paywall">
    Click **Set paywall** to apply your configuration. The paywall is now active on the link.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Edit a Paywall

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the payments section">
    Expand the **Payments** section in the Options Panel sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the edit icon">
    Click the **edit** icon next to the active paywall.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update and save">
    Change the price, the unlock type, or the recipient setting, then save your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Remove a Paywall

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the payments section">
    Expand the **Payments** section in the Options Panel sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the minus icon">
    Click the **minus (−)** icon to remove the paywall entirely.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  After you remove a paywall, access and download permissions revert to whatever you have configured in the link's standard access options (Downloads and Access toggles).
</Note>

## Test Your Paywall

Project owners and collaborators automatically bypass paywall restrictions, so you won't see the paywall when you're signed in.

<Note>
  To test the experience your clients will see, open the project link in an **incognito window** while signed out. This gives you an accurate view of the paywall as a first-time visitor.
</Note>
