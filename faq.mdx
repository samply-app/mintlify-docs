sidebarTitle: "FAQ"
description: "Quick answers to the most common questions about Samply: managing projects, playing back audio, sharing files, and getting around the app."
---

Find answers to the most common questions about using Samply below. If you can't find what you're looking for, reach out via the in-app chat and the team will help you out.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I update project settings?">
    Open the project you want to edit. Settings such as downloads, comments, and password protection are available in the **right sidebar**. If the sidebar isn't visible, click the icon in the top-right corner of the screen to open it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I reorder tracks?">
    Reordering tracks is as simple as **drag and drop**. Click and hold a track, drag it to the position you want, and release.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I download files?">
    If the project owner has enabled downloads, a download button will be visible on the project page. If you don't see a download button, contact the project owner and ask them to enable downloads for that project.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Playback sounds lossy — how do I enable lossless audio?">
    On the project, click the **Audio Options** button and toggle **Lossless** to on. This switches playback to the highest available quality for that file.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I delete a project?">
    Navigate to your **Library** view, find the project you want to remove, and delete it from there.

    <Warning>
      Deleting a project cannot be undone. Make sure you have a local copy of any files you want to keep before proceeding.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I add a collaborator to a project?">
    Open the project and use the **Share** button to invite a collaborator. Enter their email address to send them access.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I see who has viewed my project?">
    As the project owner, open the project and check the **right sidebar** — it shows a list of listeners who have accessed the project.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I change the color of my project?">
    Open the project and find the color option in the **right sidebar**. Select the color you want to apply.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I delete a song?">
    Right-click the file inside your project and choose **Delete** from the context menu.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
